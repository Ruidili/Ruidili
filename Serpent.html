Embrace of the Serpent

Summary

Directed by Ciro Guerra, 'Embrace .of the Serpent' is a Colombian-film, shot almost entirely in black and white, about the journey of an ethnographer and a botanist who are on a quest to find a rare plant called yakruna. They are accompained by indigenous shaman Karamakate, who knows the location of the palnt but is not willing to share it, even though he knows this is the only cures for Theo's-the ethnographer- disease. Although Theo is not succesful in his quest to find the plant, Evan- the botanist- does get access to it at the end of the film. The plant, which is hallucinogenic, allows Evan to experience a superconcious experience
